Who I Am

I'm a student in my third year at Cornell University studying computer science. I am interested in both high level theory and practical skills in computing. I appreciate simple design and projects with useful applications.

I also compete for Cornell's Varsity Lightweight Rowing team which has a rich tradition dating back more than one hundred years. We compete against our Ivy League counterparts and other schools on the east coast.

I grew up at the Jersey Shore and outside of rowing and studying, I enjoy cooking, hiking, and reading, and my favorite sports to follow are Major League Baseball and College Football.
